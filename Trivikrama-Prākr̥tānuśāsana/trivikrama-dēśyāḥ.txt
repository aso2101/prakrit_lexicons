These 846 words are listed under sūtra 3.4.72 of Trivikrama’s Prakrit grammar (jhāḍagās tu dēśyāḥ siddhāḥ). I have used P. L. Vaidya’s edition of 1954 (Jaina Saṁkṛti Saṁrakṣaka Saṁgha, Sholapur). Vaidya has assigned the words serial numbers that I will retain here. The Prakrit words are listed after the serial number, and their definition follows on the next line, begun with an equals sign. Cross-references to Hēmacandra’s Dēśīnāmamālā, and variant readings, both taken from Vaidya’s edition, follow.
